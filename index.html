A little about me

I think it's safe to believe that, in some unique form, everyone has experienced the moment of self-reflection in which we're faced with answering one (albeit very simple) extraordinarily massive question: What do you want to be when you grow up? Chances are, you're like me and have changed answers a few times or more.

Find a more casual photo to use!

I've always had tons of interests and hated the popular belief that you have to pick just one. As an adult, I've grown even more curious as I try to spend most of each day learning something new. I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas, where I currently live. I love traveling, camping and being outdoors. I keep my camera near me as I'm an avid photographer and naturally enjoy snapping photos of my biggest interests: friends and family, music, nature, architecture/urban design, art, travel and more.

I attended the University of Missouri and moved back to Dallas in 2014 to begin a career in digital marketing. The following two years helped me realize that branding a product simply wasn't enough. I needed a better way to tap my creativity and saw programming as the perfect opportunity. I had close to zero coding experience and was worried I'd find I might not have what it takes to be a great programmer, less make a career of it. My feelings of doubt lingered until the day I finally decided to strap myself in and take the plunge. Fast-forward to today and I can hardly believe how far I've come! I love programming and it's clear to me now that taking that leap was the best decision I've ever made.
