A little about me

Let's start with the basics: I'm a guy who likes to laugh and get to know people. I'm obsessed with music of all kinds and love going to concerts with my friends. People know me as the guy who eats a ton of food. I prefer to grab a drink at the kind of place where you can relax and converse. I'm always planning a trip to someplace where I can take photos that I'll keep forever. I seek to learn something new every day. I look for the silver lining in everything and try to cultivate positivity whenever possible.

Find a more casual photo to use!

Moving on to what I do: I'm a front-end JavaScript developer working towards becoming a full-stack web developer. My journey of learning to code began at the start of 2017 when I chose to make programming a daily practice; I signed up for FreeCodeCamp's online bootcamp and realized my passion. Diving into the programming pool to see if I'd sink or swim is still the scariest and best decision I've ever made. I still have lots of room for improvement and I'm excited to find a team that embraces my passion and eagerness to continue learning.

As a junior developer, finding that "dream job" is certainly a goal of mine, but not my own measure of success. My ambition is to continue sharpening my skills and commit myself to my newfound career & hobby. My hope that as I gain experience I'll grow into other areas such as mobile development, blockchain, data science, and machine learning. Wherever my area of focus shifts, I aim to educate and push myself towards expertise. If programming has taught me anything, it's that the only true failure is giving up.
